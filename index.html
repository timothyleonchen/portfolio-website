Hello! My name is Timothy Chen.

I am a UX/UI designer focused on improving user experiences with digital products.

In addition to my strong desire to learn and bring projects to completion, I am skilled in identifying problems and creatively finding solutions, prototyping, and making design decisions backed by user research. With a background in performing in a symphony orchestra, have extensive experience communicating in a collaborative environment and delivering aesthetically pleasing products to various audiences.

My Work

FlyFishCast

I designed a fly fishing forecasting mobile application to ease how anglers approach their endeavors on the water and to fulfill the digital gap that existed within the industry. Throughout the initial user-centric processes, such as user research and information architecture, all the way to high-fidelity prototyping and usability testing, I was the only designer on this project.

William Schettler, luthier (work-in-progress)

I am currently in the process of creating a website for Louisiana Philharmonic Orchestra’s Assistant Principal Double Bassist, William Schettler, who has expressed a need for a scheduling tool for his work as a luthier. Alongside the necessary design processes for his webpages, the core of this project is to implement a tool where his customers can book appointments to bring their instruments to his workshop.
